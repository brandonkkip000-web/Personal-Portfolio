About Me

I’m Brandon Kimutai Kiplangat — a Cyber Security and Computer Networks student with a strong passion for building secure, efficient and scalable digital systems. My work spans defensive security, network automation and game development, where I combine creativity with technical precision.

Currently pursuing my degree at Strathmore University (2024–2028), I’m continuously exploring how systems, automation and intelligent design intersect to enhance cyber resilience and performance.

Beyond academics, I co-create at Cresenty Studio — a creative tech collective where I develop interactive games and innovative tools that blend technology, art and storytelling.

I’m passionate about building secure automation scripts, experimenting with emerging security frameworks and contributing to open-source projects that empower others to learn and innovate safely online.
